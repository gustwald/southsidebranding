SOUTHSIDE BRANDING

Who are we?

We’re a web design agency that specialise in marketing people, products or companys on the web. We develop web pages that will emphasize your products, music or ideals. We’re situated in a suburb in Stockholm called Midsommarkransen. Hence the name Southside Branding, the founders grew up in the southern side of Stockholm and we like to be close to our roots.

Work

We got the privilege of working with one of our favourite artist Miike Snow and made their web page, it brought us different challenges in combining both information and making a slick design.

We made the web page for Eytys, a swedish brand that specialises on making sweet unisex shoes. They gave us free reign over the design and our team went nuts, the result turned out great and we are proud of working with a swedish brand.

One of our favourite projects was with Adidas when we made the website for their new shoe model ZX FLUX, we combined cool javascript animations with sleek design and this is to date the firms favourite project.
